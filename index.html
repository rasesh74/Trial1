1

3

Core Business Strategy

No Commission. Absolute Trust.

By taking 0% commission on orders from neighborhood retailers, Har Gali changes the dynamic from a vendor model to a true partnership. We make money alongside our local retailers instead of cutting into their thin margins.

Unit Economics & Revenue Streams

₹

1. Bulk Wholesale Arbitrage

We source consumer items directly from manufacturers in massive volume. By bypassing traditional distributor tiers, we capture high gross margins while passing better prices to retail partners.

📦

2. Proximity Micro-Fees

A minor, fixed service fee ranging from ₹10 to ₹40 is applied to transactions. This covers the underlying delivery infrastructure without altering the core price of goods.

📈

3. Hyperlocal In-App Ad Engine

Participating stores can deploy localized ad campaigns to boost visibility for high-yield inventory directly to buyers checking the app nearby.

🚀

4. Premium Feature Layer (Future)

Value-added upgrades, including predictive local stock planning, predictive inventory tooling, and custom premium visibility algorithms for growing retailers.
